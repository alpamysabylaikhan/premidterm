About Cai Haoyu

Cai Haoyu is a Chinese entrepreneur and the co-founder of miHoYo, the studio behind Honkai Impact 3rd and Genshin Impact. Under his leadership, miHoYo has become one of the most successful video game developers, known for its anime-inspired visuals, immersive worlds, and global community.

Achievements

  • Co-founded miHoYo in 2011 with fellow students
  • Released Honkai Impact 3rd (2016), gaining global success
  • Launched Genshin Impact (2020), a worldwide phenomenon
  • Promotes the vision “Tech Otakus Save the World”
